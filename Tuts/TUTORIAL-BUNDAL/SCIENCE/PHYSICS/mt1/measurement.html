Measurement Game for Kids

Practice measuring length and weight with this interactive measurement game for kids.

Your job is to measure the length and weight of various parcels so you can give them a stamp with the correct postage rate. Use the interactive scales and ruler to help you complete the challenges. Have fun with this great educational activity and enjoy all our free math games online.
