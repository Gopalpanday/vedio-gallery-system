Pythagrean Theorem(4)


Problem

Prove Pythagrean Theorem by changing area .

Applet

How to use this applet.
  • Drag red point
  • Press "Define" button.
  • Press ">" button to go next.
  • Press "<" button to go back.
  • Press ">|" button to go to the final step.
  • Press "|<"button to go to the first step.
