Pantograph

About the applet

"Pantograph" is a drawing instrument to magnify figures.
Tracing the original figure by moving the red point, we can automatically obtain the magnified figure with the pen at the blue point.

Find the ratio of magnification when the lengths of the arms are defined in the right figure.

Applet

How to use the applet

  • Drag the red point to draw the figure.
  • Press "CLS" button to clear the figure.
