Common Tangents

Some common tangents of two circles can be drawn.
You can find that the number of them varies by the condition of the distance and radii of two circles.
Using the applet of Common Tangents, try to explore this relation to the commom tangents.

Given two circles of radii a, b (a>b), with d as the distance between their centers, the following figures show the relation of them to the common tangents.
