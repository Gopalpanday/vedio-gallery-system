Billiards Game

About the game

A ball was shot at the bottom-left corner to the direction of 45 degrees from each side of billiards table. Predict which of the corner the ball will exit.

How to use the applet

Input the height and the width of the billiards table , then click "change" button.
To shot the ball, click "Start" button.

Applet
