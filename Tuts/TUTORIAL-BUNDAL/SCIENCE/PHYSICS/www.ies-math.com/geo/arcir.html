Introduction

There is a circle whose diameter is AB. We define the area of the circle is x(blue circle).
Point H is on AB.
We draw two semicircles.
One semicircle whose radius is AH, the other is BH.
We define the area of the sum of two semicircle is y(green area).
Then x = y.
