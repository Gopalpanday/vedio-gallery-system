Autonomous System (Linear_Part I)

Introduction

The figure shows Point P travelling on x-y plane.

When the velocity of a point is determined by its location (x,y) as

dx/dt = a x + b y
dy/dt = c x + d y

the system is called linearly autonomous.

Now Vx = -y and Vy = x that the orbit of P makes a circle.

Grasp the concept while observing other examples.
