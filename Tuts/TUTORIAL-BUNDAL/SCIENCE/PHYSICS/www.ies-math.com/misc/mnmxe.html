Minimum Value of Quadratic Functions

Problem

We have a quadratic function f(x)=x^2-2ax+2a+3.

(1) Find the minimam value m(a) of the function.
(2) Find the maximum value of m(a).

About the applet

The graphs of the quadratic functions having the form of f(x)=x^2-2ax+2a+3 depend on the parameter "a". The applet shows the series of the graphs in 3-dimension.
