About
Make and investigate series. In the applet screen you can drag the sequence on the left to the workspace. If an operation is used in the sequence the result is shown in the sequence on the left. This sequence can be dragged to the work space and can be used for new operations.Use the drop down menu on the sequence for different options or type in the operation on N you want (+, -, *, /, ^)
