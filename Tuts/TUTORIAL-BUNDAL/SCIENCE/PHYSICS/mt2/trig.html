Trigonometry

The applets are started by clicking the red buttons.


 
The applet Triangle and Law of Sines emphasizes that the mathematical symbols denoting lengths, angles and trigonometric functions in a triangle stand for concrete numbers. Furthermore, it illustrates (two versions of) the Law of Sines. Note that the applet does not provide a proof of the latter. For a multimedia proof see The Law of Sines by Math Education and Technology.
The applet is started from the red button in its own window.
