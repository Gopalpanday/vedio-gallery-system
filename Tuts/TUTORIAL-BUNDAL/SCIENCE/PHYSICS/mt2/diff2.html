Differentiation 2

The applets are started by clicking the red buttons.


 
Nowhere differentiable functions is an applet presenting two functions which are continuous, but not differentiable in any point. Such examples show that the functions we usually encounter just provide a very special class within the set of all functions. The applet is started from the red button in its own window.
