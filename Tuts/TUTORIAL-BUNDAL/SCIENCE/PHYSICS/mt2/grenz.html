Limiting processes


 

The tool
 
Numerical computation of sequences

helps to analyse numerical sequences and illustrates the connection between the definition of a sequence in terms of an expression and its numerical properties. It gives you a "feeling" for the possible behaviour of sequences and invites to do some experiments.
 

The tool
 
Numerical computation of series

allows to analyse a given series in terms of its sequence of partial sums. It helps understanding what the convergence of a series actually is.
 

The anecdote
 
What is a fault? A tale that makes you smile

illustrates that mathematical "faults" are often simple misunderstandings that may be identified and corrected.
