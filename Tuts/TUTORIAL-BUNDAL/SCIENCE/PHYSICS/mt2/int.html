Integration

The applets are started by clicking the red buttons.


 
In the applet Intuilively understanding the integral a function may be generated by clicking or dragging the mouse. The graph of this function is represented along with its integral. The goal is to discuss elementary connections between both graphs. This applet may be used as a test. It might also be suitable for working in small groups. It is started from the red button in its own window.
