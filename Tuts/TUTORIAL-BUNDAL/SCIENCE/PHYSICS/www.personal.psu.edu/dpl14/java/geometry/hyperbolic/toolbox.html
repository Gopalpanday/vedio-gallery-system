Hyperbolic Geometry

Download this applet for off-line viewing (includes source code)

Point
Click anywhere on the applet to create a new movable point. You may also drag any pre-existing movable point to a new position. If the mouse is over a point, the point will be highlighted.

Line
Click on any two distinct points, A and B. Draws the line through A and B.

Midpoint
Click on any two distinct points, A and B. Draws the midpoint of A and B.
